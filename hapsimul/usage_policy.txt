Wits Usage Policy for Data Generated using hapsimul and other related code

Recipients must provide evidence of ethics training in the last three years, but no ethics approval is required. Recipients should be staff members of their organisation and 
must agree to the following
a data can only be used for training and computational benchmarking purposes
b no attempt must be made to reidentify individuals
c where used for training, the recipient may provide the data to trainees but must ensure that trainees accept these conditions
d supervisors may provide the data to staff and students who report to them their same organisation
e except for the provisions of (c) and (d) no further redistribution of data is allowed
